# How to Add Your Own MP3 Files

## Step 1: Download Tamil Songs
You can download Tamil songs from:
- Your personal music collection
- Legal music platforms (with downloaded files)
- Public domain Tamil music
- Your own recordings

## Step 2: File Naming Convention
Save your MP3 files with these exact names in the `audio-samples` folder:

### Anirudh Songs:
- `aaluma_doluma.mp3` - Aaluma Doluma (Vedalam)
- `vaathi_coming.mp3` - Vaathi Coming (Master)
- `arabic_kuthu.mp3` - Arabic Kuthu (Beast)

### A.R. Rahman Songs:
- `vaseegara.mp3` - Vaseegara (Minnale)
- `hosanna.mp3` - Hosanna (VTV)
- `uyire_uyire.mp3` - Uyire Uyire (Bombay)

### Harris Jayaraj Songs:
- `kadhal_rojave.mp3` - Kadhal Rojave
- `vennilave_vennilave.mp3` - Vennilave Vennilave

### Ilaiyaraaja Songs:
- `mandram_vantha.mp3` - Mandram Vantha Thendralukku
- `chinna_chinna_aasai.mp3` - Chinna Chinna Aasai

### Other Popular Songs:
- `rowdy_baby.mp3` - Rowdy Baby (Maari 2)
- `why_this_kolaveri.mp3` - Why This Kolaveri Di

## Step 3: File Location
Place all MP3 files in:
`C:\Users\Kautham\OneDrive\Desktop\Chatbot\public\audio-samples\`

## Step 4: Test
After adding files, restart your chatbot and the songs will play directly!

## Supported Formats:
- MP3 (Best compatibility)
- WAV (High quality)
- OGG (Alternative format)

## File Size Recommendations:
- Keep files under 10MB each for fast loading
- Use good quality MP3 (128kbps or higher)
- Ensure files are not corrupted
